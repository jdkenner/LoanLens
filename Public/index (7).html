Fee Guide

Not every fee is negotiable — and that’s by design

Third-Party / Independent

Same regardless of lender

  • Title insurance (owner’s & lender’s)
  • Settlement / closing agent fee
  • Recording fees (county)
  • Transfer taxes
  • Homeowner’s insurance premium
  • Property tax escrow prepaids
  • HOA dues (if applicable)
  • Flood determination fee
Lender-Controlled & Comparable

Where lenders compete (or hide)

  • Origination / underwriting fee
  • Discount points (rate buydown)
  • Application fee
  • Rate lock fee
  • Courier / wire fee
  • Credit report fee
  • Mortgage insurance premium (PMI)
  • Interest rate & APR
  • Loan term & amortization
