What Is a Loan Estimate?

A Loan Estimate (LE) is a standardized 3-page document every mortgage lender is required by federal law to give you within 3 business days of receiving your application. It breaks down your loan terms, projected monthly payments, and estimated closing costs.

The problem? Not all fees on a Loan Estimate are equal. Some fees are set entirely by the lender — and they vary wildly from lender to lender. Others are third-party costs that are roughly the same no matter who you borrow from.

Most borrowers can’t tell the difference — and lenders know it.
